Why Generated

Designed by algorithm, finished by hand.

FACET pieces don't start on a sketchpad — they start as parametric code. We define the rules (facet count, curvature, weight distribution) and let the generator explore thousands of variations before our jewelers select and refine the one that gets printed.

The result is geometry no hand-carving process would arrive at on its own: asymmetric facets that still catch light evenly, hollow lattices that cut weight without cutting durability, and a form language that's unmistakably ours.

How It's Made

From code to carat.

01

Design

Parametric rules generate hundreds of facet variations; a jeweler selects one.

02

Print

The chosen form is 3D-printed in castable wax resin at 0.2mm resolution.

03

Cast

Lost-wax casting in solid 14K/18K gold, one piece at a time.

04

Finish

Hand-polished, set, and inspected before it ever reaches you.
